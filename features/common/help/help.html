🎯 Master Problem Patterns, Not Individual Solutions

Coding platforms like LeetCode and AlgoMonster have thousands of problems. Memorizing code line-by-line fails in interviews. AlgoRecall uses the scientific Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler (FSRS) to model your personal memory retention decay curve. It calculates exactly when you should re-solve patterns to dramatically reduce study time (saving 20–30%+ review time) while maximizing long-term retention.

📅 FSRS Spaced Repetition Engine

The Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler (FSRS) is a 17-parameter mathematical model that calculates retrievability R(t, S) = (1 + F · t / S)d. It models stability (S) and difficulty (D) independently, providing 20-30% higher retention efficiency than legacy SuperMemo (SM-2) systems.

⏱️ Key Benefit: Reduced Study Time

By dynamically expanding review intervals for patterns you know well and focusing strictly on unstable cards, FSRS reduces overall study time by 20% to 30%+ compared to traditional flashcard algorithms. You learn more patterns in significantly fewer total reviews!

Grade Your Recall Performance

Again (0%)

Complete Blackout: Couldn't recall the pattern or solution approach. The card is reset for immediate re-learning.

Hard (50%)

Struggled: Recalled the pattern with significant effort or minor errors. Spacing interval increases moderately.

Good (90%)

Optimal Recall: Remembered the solution approach in reasonable time. Interval is expanded optimally.

Easy (100%)

Trivial: Recalled instantly without hesitation. Spacing interval is set significantly further in the future.

🧠 Understanding FSRS Parameters (w0 – w16)

FSRS uses 17 coefficients to compute memory stability updates upon grading:

  • Target Retention Slider (0.75 - 0.95): Sets desired recall probability. Higher retention (95%) yields shorter intervals; lower retention (80%) spreads reviews further apart.
  • Initial Stability (w0 – w3): Base interval days for Again, Hard, Good, Easy on first review.
  • Difficulty & Stability Boosts (w4 – w10): Rate of stability increase for correct answers.
  • Lapses & Easy Bonuses (w11 – w16): Recovery deceleration upon failure and easy bonuses.
