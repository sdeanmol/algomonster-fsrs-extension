📅 How Spaced Repetition Works

FSRS is a modern spacing algorithm superior to legacy SuperMemo (SM-2) systems. It models your memory decay and customizes intervals based on how you grade your performance.

Grade Your Recall

Again

Complete blackout, or you couldn't identify the pattern. The card is scheduled for immediate re-learning.

Hard

You remembered the pattern with extreme effort or made minor mistakes. Spacing interval increases slightly.

Good

Standard recall. You remembered the solution approach in reasonable time. Interval is expanded optimally.

Easy

You knew the solution instantly. The pattern is too trivial. Spacing interval is set far in the future.

🧠 Interactive Reviews

When cards are due, you can click the notification or open the review session directly from the page widget. The review loads the problem description. Click Show Approach to view your past notes, re-solve/think through the pattern, and then select your rating to update the card's memory state.

🖍️ Text Highlighter & Notes

Highlighting critical concepts directly on documentation helps anchor visual memory. The highlighter preserves your selections persistently.

How to Use

  • Simply select any text on matched pages to open the floating palette.
  • Click any color circle to immediately apply the color overlay.
  • Click the custom wheel icon to pick an exact color, or click the red 🗑️ icon to remove a highlight.
  • All selections are serialized to the DOM and re-injected automatically when you return!

Highlighter Customization

📊 Custom Topic Weights

Are you already an expert in Greedy Algorithms but struggle with Dynamic Programming? You can customize the FSRS calculations per tag!

  1. Open **Advanced Settings** at the bottom of the popup dashboard.
  2. Enter the tag name (e.g. Dynamic Programming).
  3. Input a custom array of 17 FSRS parameter values (e.g. setting lower weights to review DP cards more frequently).
  4. Click **Save Array** to assign this weighting formula to DP tagged problems.
